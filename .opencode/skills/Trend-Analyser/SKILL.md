# Your Skill

## When to use this
<Use when the user asks:

"Why has my glucose increased?"
"Have I been improving?"
"Are there any trends?"
"What factors affect my readings?"

This skill should be used after data collection>

## Procedure
1. <Review patient metrics from the Data Collector skill.>
2. <Compare recent values against previous days.>
3. <Identify meaningful changes.>
4. <Look for potential relationships between: glucose and sleep, glucose and exercise, glucose and calories, glucose and weight, glucose and environmental factors.>
5. <Highlight notable findings.>
6. <Clearly separate: observations, possible explanations, Avoid making medical diagnoses.>
7. <Pass findings to the Advice Generator if recommendations are needed.>
