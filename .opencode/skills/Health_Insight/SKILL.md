# Health Insight

Generate personalised insights from historical records.

# Procedure
Review recent records.
Compare current day to previous days.
Identify meaningful changes.

Look for relationships between:
  sleep and activity
  sleep and risk score
  calorie intake and weight
  environment and exercise
  steps and exercise risk
Produce 2-5 personalised insights.
Explain reasoning.

# Example Insights
Lower sleep scores consistently coincide with higher exercise risk scores.
Days with more than 8,000 steps show lower risk scores.
High temperatures appear to reduce activity levels.
Increased calorie intake is associated with gradual weight gain.
